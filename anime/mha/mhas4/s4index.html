My Hero Academia Season 4

In a world where those with powers are known
as "Quirks," Izuku Midoriya has aspirations
to one day become a hero but there's a catch
-- he isn't a Quirk. After a tragic accident
involving his friend Katuski Bakugo; Midoriya
is the only one to have stepped forward to
help protect Bakugo from a villain, because
of his acts, he is given a gift by the world's
greatest hero, All Might. Now, Midoriya attends
U.A. School--a school that cultivates the next
generation of superheroes.
